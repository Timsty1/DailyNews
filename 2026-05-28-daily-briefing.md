## 5. Embodied AI — The Regulatory On-Ramp Just Opened

**Headline (announced Friday, picked up across Wed May 27 cycle)**: Beijing launched the **Humanoid Full Lifecycle Management Service Platform** under the **Humanoid Robotics and Embodied Intelligence Standardization (HEIS) committee** at MIIT. Every humanoid manufactured in China gets a **29-character digital ID code** tracked from production through recycling.

| Component | Length | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Cross-border shipment code | 2 digits | Export/import tracking |
| Manufacturer code | 4 digits | Identifies the Chinese firm |
| Product type code | 6 digits | Identifies the model family |
| Serial code | 17 digits | Unique to each robot |

- **~28,000 robots / ~200 models** already enrolled across **100+ manufacturers**.
- Read: this is the *infrastructure* layer of an industrial policy — China is treating humanoids like vehicles (VINs), not consumer electronics.
